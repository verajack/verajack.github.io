About Me

I've spent over 20 years solving complex IT challenges and building custom automation solutions that make people's lives easier. From government networks to enterprise systems, I love taking tricky problems and turning them into elegant, working solutions.

I've worked extensively with Network Engineers throughout my career, designing automation solutions that make complex network tasks simple. I'm fascinated by statistics and love combining my coding skills with data analysis.
